            cmap       = 'coolwarm'   , # changing to MEDIUM colors

            linecolor  = 'black'      , # lines between boxes
            linewidths = 0.5          ) # how thick should the lines be?


# title and displaying the plot
plt.title(label = """
Linear Correlation Heatmap for bikes_data
""")


# visualizing the data
plt.show(block = True)

The heatmap displays linear correlations between data in "bikes_data.xlsx". This heatmap uses warm colors to represent strong correlations and cool colors to indicate negative relationships. The strongest correlated variable is between Temperature and RENTALS (0.55). Hour also shows a moderate positive correlation (0.42). These are suggesting that weather customer demand depend on temperature and hours. On the other hand Humidity influence RENTALS in a negative way (-0.22), meaning it reduce customer demand for rental bike.

・Analysis Questions

1 Why are the majority of users renting bicycles?

Most customers rent bike for commuting. As mentioned above, the data shows the two peak hours during 8 AM and 6-7 PM on weekdays. These peaks much typical rush hours for public transportation, indication that bike trips are related to work and school commuting. In these hours, transportation and traffic are usually crowded. It is expected that customers want to avoid commuting in crowd and choose bike rental. renting bike might be replacing driving and public transportation for commute.

2 Are there ideal weather conditions that create high bike rental demand?

There is ideal weather conditions for bike rental. The demand hit the highest when the condition is comfortable to ride a bike. The ideal condition is time temperature is around 30 degree, and time visibility and UV index are fairly high. Also, Lower humility is preferable for bike riders. The heatmap indicates that The most influential factor is temperature. From the analysis, the ideal weather condition is a mild 30 degree warm day with clear sky and no rain.

3 Actionable steps can the company take in order to take advantage of your insights.

To take advantage of the insights, the company should consider several actions:

Improve operation during peak hours: To commute to work, most customers will begin ride from their neighborhood to office district in morning and vice versa in evening. The company could improve customer satisfaction and the number of rentals by optimizing supply of bikes in appropriate area and hours. For example, The company can track locations of bike and the number of bikes in each station, and should prepare enough bikes in suburbs area to meat high demand.

Optimizing price strategy: From the analysis, it is found that the nicer weather condition help increase customer demand for bike rental. When the company build new price strategy for bike rental service, this data should be taken into consideration. Higher price during summer when demand increases would improve profitability and lower price in winter might create new demand. However, the company need to consider other factors when consider increasing price to ensure the new price influence badly to demand.

Promotion for off-peak usage: Off-peak hours such as daytime on weekday is where the company to look at opportunities to create new demand. For example, Targeting tourists and deliver marketing and advertising to them will help the company make new revenue streamline. I would recommend to conduct marketing research to define target customer and develop market strategies to reach them.
